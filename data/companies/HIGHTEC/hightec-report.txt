founded in 1976 and headquartered in Sungai Buloh, Selangor. The company

   - Precision engineering moulds, dies, jigs, fixtures, and tools

     Poland, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Ireland), China, Japan, Thailand,
     Indonesia, Myanmar, Hong Kong, Australia

   - Investment holding and property letting
   - Rental income from commercial properties

3. PLANTATION
   - Oil palm cultivation
   - Fresh Fruit Bunches (FFB) production

4. JOINT PROPERTY DEVELOPMENT
   - Property development ventures with partners

INDUSTRIES SERVED:

- Electrical & Electronics
- Medical devices
- Consumer products
- Industrial equipment
- Furniture manufacturing

MARKET POSITION:

manufacturing with property and plantation operations, providing revenue
stability across economic cycles.
