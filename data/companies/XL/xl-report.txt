SECTION 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW & BUSINESS MODEL
================================================================================

1.1 CORPORATE PROFILE

XL Holdings Berhad, incorporated in 1998 and headquartered in Kuala Lumpur,
is a vertically integrated ornamental fish company operating through five
primary business segments:

1. Fish Farming (Core Business)
   - Commercial captive breeding of Asian Arowana
   - Stingray breeding and cultivation
   - Specialty ornamental fish species

2. Merchandise & Retail
   - Trading of ornamental fishes
   - Aquarium and accessories retail
   - Fish feed and medications

3. Edible Bird Nest
   - Trading and distribution of consumables

4. Foods
   - Manufacturing and trading of edible foods

5. Growing Crop
   - Seaweed cultivation operations

Company renamed from Xian Leng Holdings Berhad to XL Holdings Berhad in April
2021, signaling brand evolution and business diversification.

Market Capitalization: RM 230.11 million
EBITDA: RM 14.55 million
EBITDA Margin: 15.07%
Earnings Per Share (EPS): RM 0.03


1.2 OPERATIONAL FOOTPRINT

The company operates approximately four specialized fish farms and one
ornamental fish trading center:

PRIMARY BREEDING FACILITIES:

Farm 1 & 2 - Parit Sulong and Kangkar Senangar, Batu Pahat, Johor
- Focus: Commercial captive breeding of Asian Arowana and Stingray
- Setting: 70-acre hillside location with protected forest reserve
- Environment: Green vegetation, controlled natural habitat
- Species: Premium Asian Arowana, exotic Stingray varieties

Farm 3 - Sungai Suluh, Batu Pahat
- Focus: Diversified ornamental fish breeding
- Species Portfolio:
  * Japanese Koi (premium ornamental carp)
  * Anabantids (gouramis, bettas)
  * Goldfish varieties
  * Specialty tropical species

Farm 4 - Kluang, Johor
- Focus: Tropical fish mass breeding
- Species: Gourami, Pleco, Goldfish varieties
- Purpose: Volume production for retail market

RETAIL & TRADING CENTER:

XL Aquarium Megastore - Batu Pahat
- Established: 1995
- Positioning: One-stop ornamental fish center
- Offerings:
  * Local and exotic aquarium fish
  * Complete aquarium systems and accessories
  * Fish feed, medication, and maintenance supplies
  * Competitive pricing strategy
